.. _nuthreatpreventioninfo:

nuthreatpreventioninfo
===========================================

.. class:: nuthreatpreventioninfo.NUThreatPreventionInfo(bambou.nurest_object.NUMetaRESTObject,):

Represents information about Threat Prevention service/instance on NSG. This is read only entity.


Attributes
----------


- ``management_server_connection_status``: Connection status between Threat Prevention manaement Server and NSG.

- ``service_status``: Status of Threat prevention service/instance on NSG.

- ``configuration_status``: VSD Configuration status for Threat Prevention service on NSG.

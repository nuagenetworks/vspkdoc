.. _nucertificatemetadata:

nucertificatemetadata
===========================================

.. class:: nucertificatemetadata.NUCertificateMetadata(bambou.nurest_object.NUMetaRESTObject,):

An object to store metadata about an end entity's certificate.


Attributes
----------


- ``sha1_fingerprint``: The SHA1 FingerPrint of the certificate.

- ``serial``: The unique serial number of the certificate assigned by the certificate authority.

- ``not_after``: The end date of the certificate validity period.

- ``not_before``: The start date of the certificate validity period.

- ``issuer``: The distinguished name of the certificate issuer.

- ``subject``: The subject distinguished name of the certificate.
